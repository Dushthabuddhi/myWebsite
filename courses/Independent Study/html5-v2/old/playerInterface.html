Heads Up Display

The current list of user interface panels encompasses:

  • Health/Armor: The player's current health and armor level.
  • Ammunition: The available ammunition for the currently active weapon, divided into ammunition available until a reload is required, and total ammunition available, where appropriate.
  • Radar: A small circular radar indicating the locations of nearby Zombies. Other points of interest, e.g. stores, airdrop locations, active and inactive traps are also marked. Blips are color-coded to ensure that players can easily distinguish them.
  • Map: A small scale map of the entire level. Similar to the radar, it has current location, stores, taxicabs, and active traps and events marked with color coded symbols.
  • Cash/Reputation: The player's current cash balance and their respect level.
  • Alert history: A log of recent messages received through the alert panel (see below).

Other panels may be added as needed.
