Introduction

You: As a survivor of an unknown event which has caused people to go Crazy and turn into zombies, you as a player are forced to assume a leadership role in order to insure the survival of a group of people you are looking over.

Theme: Surviving the attacks of Crazies, Zombies, and mutated creatures and trying to stay alive one day at a time.

Description

Death Event is an open-world MMO RTS type of game with strong AI features to allow the users to control, command and direct their community of survivors in order make it to tomorrow...
